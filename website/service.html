Highschool Service

In hichschool I was part of NHS (National Honor Society), contributing to my community through voluntary work. On one such occasion we went out into what used to be an area of houses meant for slaves and helped rebuild/repaint the house. We had to cover the entire house in 2 fresh coats of white paint and also fix a few parts of the house that weren't as stable as they used to be. In order to stay in NHS you have to volunteer for 25 hours annually. So in total I conpleted 50 hours of community service during the 2 years of highschool that I was in the NHS. You can find the link to NHS here: https://www.nhs.us/
